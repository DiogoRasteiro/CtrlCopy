What is CRL + COPY?

CRTL + COPY offers a full plagiarism scan to submitted documents and provides not only a report about how much plagiarized the document is, but also where it was found. Everyone can use this software. It is not mandatory to have a profile in the website, since students can use it only to check levels of plagiarism and not submit the documents right after this process, giving them an opportunity to rewrite the plagiarized parts.
